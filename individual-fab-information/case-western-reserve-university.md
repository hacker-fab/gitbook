# Case Western Reserve University

### **Hacker Fab @CWRU**

**The Hacker Fab @CWRU** is a student-ran lab operating within the Materials for Opto/Electronics Research and Education (MORE) Center. Our goal is to build low-cost and accessible nanofabrication tools. Beyond equipment, CWRU focuses on digital and logic design through Tiny Tapeout.&#x20;

### **Current Fab Capabilities:**

* DIY Lithography Stepper
* Maasi Spincoater
* Tube Furnace

### **Projects in Progress:**

* DIY RF Magnetron Sputterer
* Tiny Tapeout
