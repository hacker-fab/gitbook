### **Hacker Fab @WUST**

**The Hacker Fab @WUST** operates as a student-driven research and development initiative within the Nano- and Microelectronics (**NaMi**) group. As the first university outside of North America to join the Hacker Fab network, WUST focuses on expanding hands-on access to semiconductor fabrication and characterization, with a particular emphasis on developing reliable and accessible characterization methods.

### **Tooling in use:**

* Probe Station
* DIY Tip Etcher
* DIY Lithography Stepper

### **Tooling in progress:**

* DIY Nanopositioner
* DIY Source Measure Unit (SMU)
* DIY Spin Coater
